Electrical Engineering · University of Auckland

Engineering projects,built by hand.

I'm a second-year Electrical & Electronics Engineering student interested in control systems, embedded hardware, PCB design, and practical electronics builds. This site serves as a portfolio of my personal projects, coursework, and technical skills.

I think automation, robotics, and controls are heading for a major moment globally, and I want to be building real skills now to be part of that. I also just enjoy the work itself - the theory, the hands-on debugging, and watching the two finally meet in something that actually runs.
